# PR-TOP — NotebookLM Source Brief

## Purpose

This brief is designed to make `NotebookLM` useful immediately for presentation video development, script generation, storyboard drafting, and brand-safe creative exploration.

## Non-Negotiable Brand Rule

`PR-TOP` is the external brand.

If the name `PsyLink` appears in technical or legacy materials, treat it as an internal or outdated product reference and do not use it in public-facing outputs.

## Core Brand Facts

- Brand: `PR-TOP`
- Meaning: `Practice on Top`
- Category: AI-powered practice assistant for psychologists and therapists
- Brand promise: `Care for clients. Clarity for your practice.`
- Main hero line: `Keep Your Practice on Top`
- Tone: calm, clear, respectful, professional, human
- Archetypes: `Caregiver` + `Sage`

## Audience

- private-practice psychologists
- therapists
- counselors
- therapy-style coaches
- small therapy centers

## What the Brand Solves

- too much routine and repeated communication
- difficulty maintaining continuity between sessions
- overload from notes, reminders, follow-ups, and client context
- fear that technology can make care feel impersonal
- need for structure without losing therapeutic sensitivity

## What Must Be Communicated

- this is built for therapists
- it helps between sessions
- it reduces routine
- it supports continuity
- it keeps care human
- the therapist stays in control

## Tone Rules

Use:

- calm
- warm but restrained
- structured
- thoughtful
- credible

Do not use:

- hype marketing
- bro-SaaS language
- productivity cult messaging
- AI replacement rhetoric
- manipulative urgency

## Visual Rules

Preferred:

- calm health-tech / therapist-tech
- minimal
- soft-tech
- rounded UI
- whitespace
- teal + deep ink led palette

Avoid:

- neon
- cyberpunk
- aggressive AI motifs
- cliché therapy symbols
- generic startup blobs

## Safe Messaging Phrases

- Keep Your Practice on Top
- Care for clients. Clarity for your practice.
- Less routine. More focus on care.
- Built for therapists, not technologists.
- Support between sessions.
- A calmer way to run your practice.

## Phrases to Avoid

- replace therapists
- automate therapy
- 10x your business
- dominate your niche
- magic AI
- disruption of mental healthcare

## Safe Product Support Points

These can be used as secondary proof, not as the main identity:

- therapist dashboard
- client timeline
- diary entries: text / voice / video
- therapist notes
- structured client context
- exercise sending
- session summary / transcript state
- invite code linking

```text
Your task:
Create a detailed, brand-safe presentation video brief based primarily on the brand materials, with product materials used only as supporting proof points.

Audience:
Private-practice psychologists, therapists, counselors, and small therapy centers.

The output must communicate:
- this is built for therapists
- it reduces routine
- it supports continuity between sessions
- it keeps care human
- the therapist stays in control

Tone:
Calm, clear, professional, respectful, human.
Never hypey, aggressive, manipulative, or overly focused on AI spectacle.

Visual style:
Modern health-tech / therapist-tech.
Soft-tech, structured, minimal, premium, credible.

Deliver:

5. visual direction
6. brand consistency rules
7. phrases to avoid
8. 30-second and 15-second cutdown notes
```

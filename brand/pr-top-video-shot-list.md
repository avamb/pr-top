## VO Timing Map

- `0:00-0:07` Private practice asks for presence, care, and constant context.
- `0:07-0:16` But psychologists do far more than sessions. They manage notes, follow-ups, reminders, client history, and the invisible operational weight around care.
- `0:16-0:25` PR-TOP is an AI-powered practice assistant for psychologists and therapists.
- `0:25-0:43` Built to reduce routine, support continuity between sessions, and keep care human. Keep important client context organized. Draft thoughtful follow-ups faster.
- `0:43-0:60` Bring more structure to daily practice. And stay supported, with the therapist always in control. PR-TOP does not replace professional judgment. It supports it.
- `0:60-0:75` Care for clients. Clarity for your practice. PR-TOP. Keep Your Practice on Top.

## Typography Notes

- Use large calm headlines.
- Favor short lines.
- Avoid over-animating text.
- Keep line breaks intentional and spacious.

## Editing Notes

- Prefer 8-18 frame eased transitions over hard cuts.
- Let the viewer read every headline comfortably.
- Use motion to clarify, not to impress.
- Avoid stacking too many UI ideas in one shot.

## Design Notes

- Rounded cards and panels.
- High whitespace.
- Strong deep-ink typography.
- Teal used as functional accent.
- Sage and mist for breathing room.

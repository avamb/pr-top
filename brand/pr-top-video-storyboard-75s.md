# PR-TOP — Storyboard 75s

## Overview

- **Brand:** PR-TOP
- **Format:** presentation video
- **Duration:** 75 seconds
- **Style:** calm product brand film with UI support
- **Audience:** psychologists, therapists, counselors, small therapy centers
- **Primary goal:** communicate therapist-first value, continuity, and control

## Scene 1

- **Time:** 0:00-0:07
- **Objective:** establish emotional context
- **Visuals:** soft, minimal opening with fragments of therapist workflow; notes, calendar moments, short message cues, and reflective space
- **On-screen text:** `Private practice is meaningful — and mentally heavy`
- **Voiceover:** `Private practice asks for presence, care, and constant context.`
- **Transition:** slow dissolve into layered workflow fragments
- **UI/Product element:** none or extremely abstract UI hints only
- **Brand notes:** start human-first, not software-first

## Scene 2

- **Time:** 0:07-0:16
- **Objective:** name the hidden workload
- **Visuals:** structured montage of repeated tasks: notes, reminders, follow-ups, client context, admin fragments
- **On-screen text:** `Notes` / `Follow-ups` / `Client context` / `Repeated communication`
- **Voiceover:** `But psychologists do far more than sessions. They manage notes, follow-ups, reminders, client history, and the invisible operational weight around care.`
- **Transition:** fragments begin to align into a cleaner system
- **UI/Product element:** subtle cards, lists, messages
- **Brand notes:** burden should feel real, but never chaotic or stressful in a cheap way

## Scene 3

- **Time:** 0:16-0:25
- **Objective:** introduce the brand solution
- **Visuals:** visual shift from scattered workflow to calm, coherent interface language in brand colors
- **On-screen text:** `PR-TOP`
- **On-screen text:** `Care for clients. Clarity for your practice.`
- **Voiceover:** `PR-TOP is an AI-powered practice assistant for psychologists and therapists.`
- **Transition:** smooth pull-in to brand-led hero composition
- **UI/Product element:** hero landing composition or abstract product frame
- **Brand notes:** this is the identity moment; keep it clean and premium

## Scene 4

- **Time:** 0:25-0:34
- **Objective:** show value pillar 1
- **Visuals:** client workspace and structured timeline; relevant entries arranged in a calm, readable way
- **On-screen text:** `Between-session continuity`
- **Voiceover:** `Built to reduce routine, support continuity between sessions, and keep care human.`
- **Transition:** horizontal slide into next workflow
- **UI/Product element:** unified timeline, diary entries, session markers
- **Brand notes:** continuity should be visually obvious

## Scene 5

- **Time:** 0:34-0:43
- **Objective:** show value pillar 2
- **Visuals:** communication assistance moment; follow-up message drafting or message support layout
- **On-screen text:** `Smarter communication`
- **Voiceover:** `Keep important client context organized. Draft thoughtful follow-ups faster.`
- **Transition:** masked transition through teal accent surface
- **UI/Product element:** message drafting or communication support mockup
- **Brand notes:** avoid making it look like auto-generated spam

## Scene 6

- **Time:** 0:43-0:51
- **Objective:** show value pillar 3
- **Visuals:** therapist dashboard and structured task surface; less clutter, clearer flow
- **On-screen text:** `Less routine admin`
- **Voiceover:** `Bring more structure to daily practice.`
- **Transition:** soft zoom to broader practice overview
- **UI/Product element:** therapist dashboard, notes, tasks, exercises
- **Brand notes:** emphasize calm organization, not productivity obsession

## Scene 7

- **Time:** 0:51-0:60
- **Objective:** build trust and control
- **Visuals:** reviewed suggestion states, approval framing, subtle shield motif, protected client workspace
- **On-screen text:** `Supportive AI — with the therapist in control`
- **Voiceover:** `And stay supported, with the therapist always in control. PR-TOP does not replace professional judgment. It supports it.`
- **Transition:** fade into brand lockup
- **UI/Product element:** controlled suggestion flow, secure workspace framing
- **Brand notes:** this is mandatory; it resolves the audience's likely objection

## Scene 8

- **Time:** 0:60-0:75
- **Objective:** close with clear promise and CTA
- **Visuals:** final polished hero scene with logo/wordmark, light product support, and CTA lockup
- **On-screen text:** `Keep Your Practice on Top`
- **On-screen text:** `Start Free Trial`
- **On-screen text:** `See How It Works`
- **Voiceover:** `Care for clients. Clarity for your practice. PR-TOP. Keep Your Practice on Top.`
- **Transition:** clean hold on end frame
- **UI/Product element:** minimal landing hero or wordmark-led end card
- **Brand notes:** end with confidence and calm, not sales pressure

## Production Notes

- Keep edit rhythm steady and restrained.
- Let typography breathe.
- Use brand palette consistently.
- UI should support the story, not dominate it.
- Avoid fake testimonials unless real approvals exist.
- Keep PR-TOP as the only public-facing brand name.
